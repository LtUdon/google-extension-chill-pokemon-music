MusicDex Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Data Collection

MusicDex does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Third-Party Services

MusicDex streams music metadata and audio from the Jamendo API. Requests made to Jamendo are subject to Jamendo's Privacy Policy.

Permissions

The tabs permission is used only to open the Jamendo source page when requested by the user. No tab data is read or stored.

Contact

For questions, open an issue on the GitHub repository.
